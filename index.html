TLCshare
TLCshare is an all-encompassing information-management platform that places caregivers, families and medical professionals on the same page to improve the quality of care and life of a dependent loved one.
Caregivers can share their experiences and information with other users.
Users also have access to a private area with helpful tools, such as: Todo lists; Medication list; Addresses and contacts; and more...
By sharing their sign-in information with co-caregivers, family or friends - families can share information and tasks.

MIDDLEGROUND
This was a group project with two other students at NYCDA.
A, who would like to meet a friend, or business contact, can find the geographical middleground between their two locations. Once the middleground is established, transit information is available, and the user can also choose to pick places to go for the meet-up - such as a cafe, bar, museum, etc., and use certain key terms for the search.
